  • DCMI_FLAG_LINEMI: Line Masked flag mask
  • DCMI_FLAG_HSYNC: HSYNC flag mask
  • DCMI_FLAG_VSYNC: VSYNC flag mask
  • DCMI_FLAG_FNE: Fifo not empty flag mask
